Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the ingredients used in your soft drinks?

Our soft drinks are made with fresh and natural ingredients sourced directly from African farmers. Each flavor has a unique blend of fruits and spices that capture the authentic taste of Africa.

Where are your soft drinks made?

Our soft drinks are made in our factory in Nigeria, where we combine traditional African recipes with modern manufacturing techniques to produce high-quality and flavorful beverages.

Do you offer any sugar-free options?

Yes, we offer a sugar-free version of our most popular flavor. It has the same great taste but without the added sugar

How are your soft drinks packaged?

Our soft drinks are packaged in eco-friendly glass bottles that preserve the freshness and taste of our beverages. We also use recyclable materials for our packaging to minimize our impact on the environment.

Can I order your soft drinks online?

Yes, you can order our soft drinks directly from our website. We ship nationwide and offer free shipping for orders over N20,000.

Do you offer any discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us for more information.

How long does it take for my order to arrive?

Orders typically take 3-5 business days to arrive. We'll provide you with a tracking number once your order has been shipped so you can easily follow its progress.

What is your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 14 days of receiving it. We'll be happy to offer a refund or exchange as long as the product is returned in its original condition. For more clarity please refer to Returns And Refunds

Are your soft drinks available in stores?

At the moment, our soft drinks are not available in stores. However, we are actively working on expanding our distribution channels and making our products more widely available to customers like you.

Can I become a distributor of your soft drinks?

Yes, we're always looking for new distribution partners. Please contact us to learn more about our distribution program and how to become a distributor.
